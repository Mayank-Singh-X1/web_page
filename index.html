About Brawl Stars

Brawl Stars is a popular mobile multiplayer online battle game developed by Supercell. Released globally in December 2018, it combines elements of strategy, action, and team-based combat. The game features a variety of characters called "Brawlers," each with unique abilities, attacks, and supers (special moves).
